About

I am a Mechanical Engineer currently pursuing my M.Sc. in Materials Engineering at RWTH Aachen University, Germany — one of Europe's leading technical universities.

My background spans computational mechanics (FEA/CFD), applied R&D in the electronics manufacturing industry, and academic research in thermal engineering, combustion, and structural analysis. I bring hands-on simulation experience with ANSYS, ABAQUS, and COMSOL, alongside industry expertise from product engineering roles at Samsung & Hisense manufacturing facilities.

I am passionate about bridging rigorous simulation methods with real-world engineering challenges — particularly in lightweight structures, functional materials, and sustainable energy systems.
